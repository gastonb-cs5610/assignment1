About Captured.

Giving back the social in social media.

Captured is about giving you back your freedom, while your pages never have to miss a moment. We match you with a nearby photographer who will capture your event. The social media specialist will be in charge of posting to your social media accounts -- Snapchat, Instagram, Facebook, Boomerang, Twitter-- letting you get back to enjoying the party. Your social media specialist will create hashtags, consult with you about captions, and coordinate group photos. Never again does Tall Tracy's head need to get cut out of the selfie. Never again do you need to get out of the pool to go get your phone. Captured links you with passionate and creative local talent who's social media pages you can browse before selecting. Even if you don't remember the party, we will capture it for you.
